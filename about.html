About

I feel like chemistry is a hard subject to understand thoroughly, especially for beginners and someone who self-study it. It does not only contain tons of hard-to-understand concepts, theories, but also equally masochistic calculations. Without guidances, it is quite easy to get lost in the vastly endless realm of chemistry. I studied basic chemistry from teachers since I was 12. I start getting interested and ambitious about it back then. I wanted to dive much deeper into it when I was 15, but I feel so lost and I do not know where to start from. There was literally no one to guide me through, and the online resources were limited back then. By the time I discovered the effective ways to master chemistry, I was already an undergrad... So, I decided to create this website to share my knowledge and resources that I found useful to study, covering every levels of chemistry. You will find some guides, textbook recommendations, exams past papers, and other useful resources here. I am Thai so a lot of the content is based on Thailand's curriculum, but I believe it is still useful for everyone around the world.
